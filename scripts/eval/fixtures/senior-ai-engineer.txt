Senior AI Engineer (LLM Applications)
Nimbus Data Sp. z o.o. — Kraków / hybrid

About the role

We are building a document intelligence platform used by insurers to process
claims. You will own the retrieval and generation layer end to end, from ingestion
pipelines through evaluation harnesses to production monitoring.

Requirements

- 5+ years of commercial software engineering experience
- Strong TypeScript and Node.js; you are comfortable owning a service in production
- Hands-on experience building RAG systems: chunking strategies, embeddings, vector
  databases (we use pgvector on Postgres)
- Experience with agentic workflows and tool-calling patterns
- Practical knowledge of LLM evaluation: building test sets, LLM-as-judge, tracking
  regressions across prompt versions
- Solid understanding of system design and distributed systems fundamentals
- Experience with PostgreSQL, including query optimisation
- Comfortable with algorithmic problem solving and data structures

Nice to have

- Python for data pipelines
- Experience with observability tooling (we use OpenTelemetry)
- Kubernetes and infrastructure-as-code
- Prior work in insurance or fintech domains

Interview process

Four stages: recruiter screen, technical interview covering system design and
coding, a practical take-home on retrieval quality, and a final behavioural round
with the engineering director.

What we offer

- 25 000 - 32 000 PLN net B2B depending on experience
- Hybrid, two days per week in the Kraków office
- Annual training budget and conference attendance
